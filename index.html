Smart cube to letters

Type by twisting the cube.

Connect through the smart cube protocol, then map each move into a letter. Use only quarter turns while typing. U moves forward through the dictionaries, U' moves backward, and the typing orientation is always white on top with green on the front.

Needed when Chrome cannot read the cube MAC automatically. Some smart cubes may require manual MAC entry.

Idle. Open this page in Chrome or Edge, connect the cube, then hold it white-up and green-front before typing.
